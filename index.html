Gymotion

Mission

We make use of the wasted commuting time and integrate minimal fitness workout into busy everyday life of commuters

We are the first corporate commuter fitness bus

We equipped our fitness bus with compact elliptical devices for easy daily workout!

We offer a corporate shuttle service and charge companies a monthly fee

We help companies to get more productive and motivated employees and we help employees to get fit and healthy again, without compromising their busy schedule

Our vision

We want to make minimum recommend workout accessible in all means of transportation: plane, train, autonomous car! We want to spread this healthy way of commuting globally and integrate our fitness concept into public transportation!
